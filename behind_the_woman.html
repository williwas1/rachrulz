What you should know about the queen

When I was in university in my final year, a friend told me about a local drag show happening in the nearby pub where the cocktails were (and probably still are) plentiful and flavourful, I put on a stringy black wig and some smeared red lipstick and lipsynced to "Hit me baby one more time" by Britney Spears, it was the most fun despite probably being total rubbish.


When I saw the transformation some of these queens had gone through from no makeup to ALL the makeup, I knew a wig and some lipstick was not good enough and I decided to try it out properly. I came up with the name "Rachael Starla" on the fly, I simply combined my favourite girl name, Rachael, and combined it with the name of Muscle Man's girlfriend from Regular Show, Starla.

After that first night, I picked up some cheap makeup and borrowed some dresses from another friend who was a crossdresser, I watched some youtube videos and had a good friend show me some techniques, I found myself becoming more enthusiastic as I learned more and went on nights out in university while dressed up, being told I was gorgeous was cathartic.

And thus, Rachael Starla was spawned! Like Barbies discontinued, lower rent, gruff equivalent: Butchie
